### 1. ❤️ **Wishlist System**
**What It Does:**
- Users can save favorite products for later
- Wishlist persists in localStorage
- Visual heart button on all products
- Wishlist count badge in navbar

**Why It Wins:**
- Increases user engagement
- Encourages repeat visits
- Builds customer loyalty
- Shows advanced JavaScript knowledge

**How to Use:**
- Click 🤍 Save button on any product
- Heart turns red ❤️ when saved
- Click again to remove
- Wishlist count updates in real-time
